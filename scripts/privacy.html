Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data, including what we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

  • Location data to help locate nearby bikes
  • Payment information processed through secure gateways

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, including booking bikes, locating stations, and processing payments.

Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your data at any time through your account settings or by contacting support.
