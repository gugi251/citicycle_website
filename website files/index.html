About Us

Making commuting easy and accessible for everyone. Whether you're commuting to work, exploring local attractions, or just enjoying a leisurely ride, CitiCycle is here to support your journey.

How It Works

  1. Join: Sign up via our mobile app.
  2. Pick: Find a bike at the nearest station.
  3. Locate: Use the map to view available bikes.
  4. Pay: Choose a plan or pay per ride.
  5. Unlock: Unlock a bike using the app.
  6. Enjoy: Ride freely and enjoy the city.
  7. Return: Park the bike at any CitiCycle station.

Bike Stations

Our bike stations are conveniently located throughout Bristol, including popular spots like Temple Meads, Cabot Circus, and University of Bristol. Whether you're commuting or exploring, there’s always a CitiCycle station nearby.
